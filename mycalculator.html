SUM:

Enter a positive whole number.
This will run the Collatz function on all numbers from 1 to the number given.
If even divide by 2 if odd multiply by 3 and add 1. repeat until we have reached 1.
One canvas will tell what number took the most iteration/steps before reaching 1.
And the other will dispaly what achieved the highest number before reaching 1.

This show the journey of the one with most steps

This show the journey of the one that reached the highest
